# Shot Unit

## Definition

A shot unit is the smallest production unit that can be planned, generated, edited, and reviewed.

## Required Fields

- Shot ID.
- Scene ID.
- Duration target.
- Dramatic purpose.
- Visual description.
- Character state.
- Camera framing and movement.
- Continuity requirements.
- Prompt package.
- Generated asset path.
- Edit note.
- QA status.

## Principle

For AI video, a vague scene description creates unstable output. A shot unit gives the model a narrower target and gives the editor a controllable assembly block.

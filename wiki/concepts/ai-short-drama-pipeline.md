# AI Short Drama Pipeline

## Definition

The AI short drama pipeline is the end-to-end process for turning an idea into a finished short narrative video using AI-assisted production.

## Stages

| Stage | Output | Primary Expert |
|---|---|---|
| Premise | One-sentence dramatic promise | [[script-expert]] |
| Beat outline | Hook, escalation, reversal, payoff | [[script-expert]] |
| Scene cards | Scene goals, conflict, emotional turn | [[director-expert]] |
| Shot list | Shot-by-shot visual plan | [[storyboard-expert]] |
| Visual control | Character, setting, style, references | [[continuity-expert]] |
| Prompt packs | Text/image/video generation prompts | [[prompt-expert]] |
| Clip generation | Candidate video shots | [[prompt-expert]] |
| Assembly edit | Rough cut and timing | [[editing-expert]] |
| Sound pass | Dialogue, music, ambience, effects | [[sound-expert]] |
| QA | Continuity, story clarity, technical quality | [[continuity-expert]] |
| Delivery | Export, subtitles, metadata | [[editing-expert]] |

## Principle

Do not jump from script directly to AI video generation. Convert story into controllable production units first. The [[shot-unit|shot unit]] is the bridge between creative intent and AI execution.

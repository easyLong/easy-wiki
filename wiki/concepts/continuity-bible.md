# Continuity Bible

## Definition

A continuity bible is a control document that locks recurring facts and visual rules for a short drama project.

## Contents

- Character names, age range, appearance, costume, voice, personality, and emotional arc.
- Locations, layout, lighting logic, and key props.
- Visual style, color palette, aspect ratio, lens language, and genre references.
- Timeline and story facts.
- Forbidden changes, such as hairstyle drift or unexplained costume swaps.

## Use In AI Production

Before generating any shot, prompts should reference the relevant continuity bible entries. After generation, the [[continuity-expert]] reviews whether the output violated locked details.

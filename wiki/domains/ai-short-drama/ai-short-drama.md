# AI Short Drama

## Definition

AI short drama is a short-form narrative video production workflow where AI tools assist or generate parts of the script, storyboard, images, video clips, voice, music, subtitles, and edit decisions.

The production goal is not just "generate video." The goal is to produce a coherent dramatic experience with clear conflict, controlled visual continuity, and audience-retaining rhythm.

## Core Production Logic

AI short drama should be managed at the [[shot-unit|shot unit]] level. Each shot has a narrative purpose, visual design, continuity requirements, prompt package, generated assets, edit notes, and QA result.

The durable control documents are:

- [[continuity-bible|Continuity Bible]]
- [[scene-card-template|Scene Card]]
- [[shot-list-template|Shot List]]
- [[review-checklist-template|Review Checklist]]

## Recommended Workflow

Use [[ai-short-drama-production-workflow]] as the default pipeline:

```text
premise -> beat outline -> scene cards -> shot list -> prompt packs -> generation -> edit -> sound -> QA -> delivery
```

## Open Questions

- Which audience and platform is the short drama targeting?
- Is the format vertical, horizontal, or square?
- What is the expected duration per episode?
- Are characters realistic, stylized, anime, cinematic, or hybrid?
- Which AI tools will be used for image, video, voice, and editing?

# AI Short Drama Domain

## Purpose

This domain stores the durable knowledge system for AI-assisted short drama production. It links expert research, production workflows, reusable templates, and project lessons.

## Current Domain Scope

Included:

- Short-form scripted drama.
- AI-assisted script, storyboard, image/video generation, voice, sound, subtitles, and editing.
- Expert-role thinking for production quality.
- Project workflows and postmortems.

Not yet included:

- Full-length film production.
- Live-action production logistics at professional crew scale.
- Paid media buying and distribution operations.

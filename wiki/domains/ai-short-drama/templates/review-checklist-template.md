# Review Checklist Template

## Story

- [ ] Opening creates immediate curiosity or pressure.
- [ ] Main character's want is clear.
- [ ] Conflict is visible.
- [ ] Each scene changes the situation.
- [ ] Ending creates payoff or forward pull.

## Visual Continuity

- [ ] Character face and hairstyle are stable.
- [ ] Costume is consistent or story-motivated.
- [ ] Props remain consistent.
- [ ] Location layout is understandable.
- [ ] Lighting and time of day are coherent.

## Shot And Edit

- [ ] Shot order guides attention.
- [ ] Cut points feel motivated.
- [ ] No dead seconds.
- [ ] Reaction shots are present where emotion changes.
- [ ] Inserts or audio bridges hide unavoidable AI mismatch.

## Audio

- [ ] Dialogue is intelligible.
- [ ] Voice identity is stable.
- [ ] Music supports emotional turns.
- [ ] Ambience matches location.
- [ ] Subtitles are readable and timed correctly.

## Delivery

- [ ] Aspect ratio matches platform.
- [ ] Export length is correct.
- [ ] Opening frame is strong.
- [ ] Captions and metadata are ready.

# AI Short Drama Expert Map

## Purpose

This page decomposes AI short drama production into expert roles. It is the search map for collecting expert ideas and the execution map for doing real projects.

## Search Order

For the current AI short drama wiki, search and ingest sources in this order:

1. Story and script.
2. Storyboard and visual grammar.
3. Editing and retention.
4. Directing and performance.
5. Cinematography.
6. Sound design.
7. Continuity and script supervision.
8. AI video prompting.
9. Platform packaging.
10. Production management.

## Why This Order

Story, storyboard, and editing come first because they determine whether generated assets will be useful. Prompting comes after shot design because prompts should execute decisions, not replace them.

## Next Sources To Search

- Screenwriting: experts on short-form hooks, scene conflict, and serial cliffhangers.
- Storyboard: story artists on staging, clarity, and storyreels.
- Editing: editor interviews on pacing and emotional cuts.
- Cinematography: ASC interviews on visual grammar for mobile-readable images.
- Continuity: script supervision and AI character consistency.
- Platform packaging: YouTube/TikTok/Shorts title, opening frame, retention, and thumbnail guidance.

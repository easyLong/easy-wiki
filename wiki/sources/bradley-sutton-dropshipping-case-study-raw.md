# How I Sold $350K on Amazon in 3 Months Using Drop Shipping

## Summary

Helium 10 38 minute read April 3, 2021 January 12, 2024 There are so many different ways to make money selling on Amazon. For many reasons, dropshipping on Amazon is often considered to be a “gateway” platform for sellers looking to get into e-commerce. That’s because there’s a relatively low cost to get started, you can easily “test” a variety of products, and it’s simple to scale up once you’ve found the right product to sell. Of course, there are also disadvantages to drop shipping. Today on the Serious Sellers Podcast, Helium 10’s Director of Training and Chief Evangelist, Bradley Sutton takes us through a drop shipping case study that he recently conducted. He says that his goal is to make sure that Helium 10 users (as well as the general online selling community) receive the most up-to-date information from ground zero.

# Pixar / Khan Academy - The Art of Storytelling

## Summary

Pixar and Khan Academy present storytelling as an iterative process combining character, structure, visual language, film grammar, pitching, feedback, storyboarding, storyreels, and editorial timing.

## Useful Ideas

- Story structure organizes information to shape engagement and emotional impact.
- Visual language uses line, shape, space, motion, tone, color, size, and framing to communicate ideas and emotions.
- Film grammar combines framing, staging, camera motion, and editing.
- Shot choice should answer what the audience needs to know and what the audience should feel.
- Storyreels turn boards into a rough video form with dialogue, sound, music, and timing.
- Pixar's process iterates through storyboarding, pitching, feedback, re-boarding, and re-pitching.

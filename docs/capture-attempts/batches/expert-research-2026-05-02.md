# Expert Research Registry - 2026-05-02

This file records the first external sources collected to test the LLM Wiki workflow for AI short drama production. It stores links and brief notes only, not full copyrighted source text.

## Sources

### Pixar / Khan Academy - The Art of Storytelling

- URL: https://www.khanacademy.org/computing/pixar/storytelling
- Role coverage: script, storyboard, visual language, film grammar, pitching, storyreels.
- Useful idea: story is iterated through storyboards, pitching, feedback, re-boarding, storyreels, and editorial timing.

### Pixar - Pixar in a Box

- URL: https://www.pixar.com/pixar-in-a-box
- Role coverage: official context for Pixar/Khan Academy tutorials.
- Useful idea: Pixar frames filmmaking as an interdisciplinary process combining story, art, technology, and production.

### Walter Murch - Rule of Six

- URL: https://www.studiobinder.com/blog/walter-murch-rule-of-six/
- Supporting URL: https://cloud.wikis.utexas.edu/wiki/display/rtf318/Murch%27s%2BRule%2Bof%2BSix
- Role coverage: editing.
- Useful idea: cuts should prioritize emotion, story, rhythm, eye trace, screen plane, then spatial continuity.

### YouTube Help - Audience Retention

- URL: https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/9314415?hl=en
- Role coverage: editing, retention analysis.
- Useful idea: intros, top moments, spikes, and dips can be used as feedback signals for revising pacing and structure.

### Randy Thom - Designing A Movie For Sound

- URL: https://filmsound.org/articles/designing_for_sound.htm
- Role coverage: sound design.
- Useful idea: sound should influence story and structure early, not be pasted on after picture lock.

### DGA Interview - Dan Attias

- URL: https://www.dga.org/craft/dgaq/issues/1803-summer-2018/dga-interview-dan-attias
- Role coverage: directing, actor motivation, prep, staging, tone.
- Useful idea: the director holds the whole story, digs for universal stakes, and grounds staging in character motivation.

### OpenAI Help - Creating videos with Sora

- URL: https://help.openai.com/en/articles/12460853
- Role coverage: AI video prompting.
- Useful idea: prompts can specify subject/setting, camera/motion, look/pacing, and audio intent; timing and small casts matter.

### OpenAI API - Video generation with Sora

- URL: https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/video-generation/
- Role coverage: AI video prompting, API workflow.
- Useful idea: effective prompts describe shot type, subject, action, setting, lighting, camera, and motion.

### Runway - Gen-3 Alpha Prompting Guide

- URL: https://help.runwayml.com/hc/en-us/articles/30586818553107-Gen-3-Alpha-Prompting-Guide
- Role coverage: AI video prompting.
- Useful idea: text prompts should be clear, positive, and structured around camera movement, scene, subject, and details.

### Runway - Image to Video Prompting Guide

- URL: https://help.runwayml.com/hc/en-us/articles/48324313115155
- Role coverage: AI video prompting.
- Useful idea: image-to-video prompts should focus on what happens: motion, camera work, and temporal progression.
